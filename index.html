Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Home Loan? ‚ñº

A home loan is a form of financial support that helps you buy, build, or renovate your dream home without paying the full amount upfront. You borrow the required amount and repay it in easy monthly EMIs over a set tenure, along with interest.

How to Apply for a Home Loan at Piramal Finance? ‚ñº

You can apply online through our website, visit a branch, or call our customer care. Our team will guide you through the entire process.

What documents are required for a home loan? ‚ñº

Basic documents include identity proof, address proof, income proof, property documents, and bank statements. The exact list depends on your employment type and loan amount.
